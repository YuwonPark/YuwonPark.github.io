How do we give educational pressure to teenagers?

These are the best ways to give educational pressure to teens:

  • Setting expectations for academic achievement based on famous geniuses such as Einstein or Leonardo Da Vinci.
  • Making sure that they experience every one of academic resources available such as tutoring, counseling, and academic support programs.
  • Don't give time for rest, relaxation, and extracurricular activities since it's just a waste of time.
  • Form the atmosphere of competition between them and other students.
  • Give advice or feedback on everything they do so they can improve as quickly as possible.
